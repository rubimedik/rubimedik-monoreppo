Features

Everything you need,
in one platform.

From blood donation to telemedicine consultations, RubiMedik integrates every aspect of modern healthcare access.

Blood Donation & Requests

Request specific blood types with urgency levels, find nearby partner hospitals with available stock, and book donation appointments — all in real time.

  • Real-time blood availability tracking
  • Smart matching algorithm
  • Donation history & scheduling

Telemedicine Consultations

Connect with verified specialists via secure voice and video calls from the comfort of your home.

Smart Referral System

Get referred to the right specialist or hospital based on your symptoms, location, and urgency level.

Specialist Directory

Browse verified doctors by specialization, location, and availability. View credentials, ratings, and book instantly.

Personalized Dashboards

Role-based dashboards for patients, donors, specialists, and hospitals with real-time analytics and tracking.

Secure & Compliant

End-to-end encryption, role-based access control, and full compliance with health data regulations to keep your data safe.

Smart Notifications

Push notifications, in-app alerts, and email/SMS for appointment reminders, blood match updates, and referral status changes.

Medical Records Access

Securely share medical history with specialists (with consent). Doctors can upload consultation notes and prescriptions.

How It Works

Simple steps to
better healthcare.

Whether you need blood, want to donate, or seek specialist care — RubiMedik makes it effortless.

01

Sign Up & Verify

Create your account, choose your role (patient, specialist, or hospital), and complete your profile. Specialists and hospitals go through a quick verification process.

02

Request or Offer Care

Need blood? Request it. Want to donate? Register as a donor. Need a specialist? Search the directory and submit a referral request with your symptoms.

03

Get Matched & Connected

Our smart algorithm matches your blood request with available stock, or connects you with the right specialist. Book your appointment or join a teleconsultation instantly.

04

Track & Follow Up

Monitor your referral status, donation history, and upcoming appointments through your personalized dashboard. Get notified at every step of the way.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things people ask about RubiMedik.

RubiMedik is an integrated healthcare platform that connects patients, blood donors, medical specialists, and hospitals. It enables seamless blood donation coordination, telemedicine consultations, and medical referrals — all in one app.

Simply sign up as a patient, go to the Blood Donation module, select "Request Blood", specify the blood type and urgency level, and choose a nearby partner hospital. Our algorithm will match your request with available blood stock and notify you when a match is found.

You can search for verified specialists by specialization and location, submit a referral request with your symptoms, and schedule an appointment. Once confirmed, you can join a secure voice or video consultation directly from the app. Your medical history (with your consent) is shared with the specialist for better diagnosis.

Absolutely. RubiMedik uses industry-standard security including OAuth2 authentication, 256-bit encryption for data at rest and in transit, role-based access control, and strict privacy policies. Your medical data is only shared with healthcare providers with your explicit consent.

Specialists must upload their professional credentials (medical license, certifications), and hospitals must provide institutional accreditation documents. Our admin team reviews and verifies all submissions before granting verified status, ensuring only trusted healthcare providers are on the platform.

Yes! When you sign up as a patient, you have the option to also register as a blood donor. You can opt into blood donation, healthcare services, or both. You'll be able to track your donation history and receive notifications when your blood type is in demand.

RubiMedik is free to download and use for patients and donors. Specialists and hospitals may have premium features available through subscription plans. Blood donation coordination is always free for all users.

RubiMedik is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). We also offer a web dashboard for specialists and hospital administrators to manage their activities from a desktop browser.
