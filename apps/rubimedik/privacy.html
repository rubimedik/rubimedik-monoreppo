Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

RubiMedik collects personal information including your name, email, phone number, medical profile (blood type, health details), and location data to provide our services. We also collect usage data and device information for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to match blood requests, facilitate specialist referrals, schedule appointments, and communicate important health updates. Medical data is only shared with healthcare providers with your explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We implement 256-bit encryption for data at rest and in transit, OAuth2 authentication, and role-based access controls. Our systems undergo regular security audits and comply with relevant health data regulations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Medical information is shared only with verified specialists and hospitals upon your consent. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can withdraw consent for data sharing and request a copy of your data through the app settings.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@rubimedik.com.
