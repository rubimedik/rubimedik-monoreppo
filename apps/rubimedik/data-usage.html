Data Usage Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At RubiMedik, we take data privacy and transparency very seriously. This Data Usage Policy outlines how we use the medical and personal data you share with us to provide a seamless healthcare experience.

1. Purpose of Data Collection

The primary reason we collect your data is to facilitate blood donations and medical consultations. Specifically, we use your data to match patients in need of blood with eligible donors and hospitals.

2. Medical Information

Your medical history, blood type, and health conditions are strictly confidential. We only share this information with medical specialists if you give us permission to do so during a telemedicine referral or consultation.

3. Location and Real-time Data

To help you find the nearest blood donor or partner hospital, our platform may request access to your location. This data is only used during active requests and is never sold to third parties.

4. Anonymized Analytics

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our platform’s efficiency. For example, we might analyze trends in blood type需求 to better inform partner hospitals and blood banks.

5. Data Security Measures

We apply high-security standards, including 256-bit encryption for data at rest and in transit. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to provide you with healthcare services.

6. Your Control

You can view, update, or delete your health records through the RubiMedik app settings at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all personal medical data is permanently erased from our active databases.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding how we use your data, please contact our data projection officer at privacy@rubimedik.com.
